INTERNATIONAL AFFAIRS

CPDS has partnered with reputed international universities to help students in getting international opportunities in study and research. It has the memorandum of understanding with different international universities who offer credit transfer, scholarship, Ph.D. opportunities to SEU students. CPDS also provides guidance and assistance for following issues:

-          Maintaining liaison with the partner foreign universities

-          Advising and supporting SEU students for their higher education with the partner Universities and others

-          Coordinating student exchange programs

-          Helping students find out scholarship opportunities among the partner universities and beyond
